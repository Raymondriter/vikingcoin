Information

VikingCoin or VC is a senior design project created by Raymond Riter, Safaldeep Nehal, Madhav Pyakurel, and Johnnie Gilkerson.
It is a Cryptocurrency for use on school campuses as a currency internally. It is to be used to gain involvement in activates and reward those that are. By being involved you will make passive income by going to 'sponsered' events.
These coins may then be burned so that next time you go to an event you may get a big bonus!
